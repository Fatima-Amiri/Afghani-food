Shor Chai

Shor Chai, also known as Shor Chai Badakhshi, is a traditional dish from the northeastern region of Afghanistan, offering a unique variation of the classic milk tea. In contrast to the sweet versions found in India and Pakistan, Shor Chai is refined with salt and crushed walnuts or pistachios.

Bolani

Bolani is a stuffed flatbread from the Afghan cuisine. It is usually filled with a mix of potatoes, cilantro, and green peppers, but it can also be filled with pumpkin, red lentils, or chives. It is usually served with a green chutney and enjoyed as a side dish, or as a snack with a cup of tea.
